How It Works

Growing your own mushrooms has never been easier. Our kits come with everything you need for a successful harvest in just a few simple steps.

1

Open Your Kit

Unbox your mushroom growing kit and find your substrate block, humidity tent, and detailed instructions.

2

Set Up

Place your kit in the perfect spot (we'll help you find it) and prepare it for growth with a simple misting.

3

Watch Them Grow

In just 7-14 days, you'll see tiny mushrooms (pins) form and grow to harvest size.

4

Harvest & Enjoy

Pick your mushrooms at peak freshness and enjoy them in your favorite recipes!
