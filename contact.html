Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping typically take? +

We process orders within 1-2 business days. Shipping times vary by location:

  • USA: 3-7 business days via USPS or FedEx
  • Canada: 7-14 business days
  • International: 10-21 business days (varies by country)

Expedited shipping options are available at checkout. During peak seasons or holidays, processing times may be slightly longer.

What's your return policy? +

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase for unopened kits in their original condition. For opened kits, we offer store credit within 14 days of purchase if at least 50% of the product remains unused.

Note: Due to the nature of our products, we cannot accept returns for mushroom cultures or spores once they've been shipped. Please inspect your shipment immediately upon arrival and contact us within 48 hours if there are any issues.

Do you ship internationally? +

Yes, we ship to many countries worldwide. However, some products may be restricted in certain locations due to local laws regarding mushroom cultivation. International customers are responsible for:

  • All customs duties, taxes, and fees
  • Ensuring products are legal in their country
  • Any additional shipping charges if packages are refused by customs

Please check your local regulations before ordering. We cannot refund orders that are seized by customs.

How do I store my mushroom growing kit? +

Proper storage is essential for maintaining kit viability:

  • Unopened kits: Store in a cool, dark place (50-70°F / 10-21°C). Refrigeration can extend shelf life but isn't required for most kits.
  • Opened kits: Follow the specific instructions included with your kit. Most require consistent moisture and indirect light.
  • Liquid cultures/spores: Always refrigerate (35-45°F / 2-7°C) and use within recommended timeframe.

Never freeze your kits or expose them to extreme temperatures. Each kit includes detailed storage instructions.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept all major payment methods for your convenience:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover)
  • PayPal
  • Apple Pay
  • Google Pay

For wholesale orders over $1,000, we also accept bank transfers (contact our sales team for details). All transactions are securely processed through our PCI-compliant payment system.

Can I visit your facility in person? +

We occasionally offer facility tours for educational and wholesale partners, but generally don't have a public storefront due to the sterile nature of our production facilities. However, you're welcome to:

  • Schedule a virtual tour with our team
  • Visit us at local farmers markets (check our events calendar)
  • Attend one of our monthly mushroom cultivation workshops

For wholesale buyers and educational institutions, private tours can be arranged by contacting sales@homegrownshrooms.com at least two weeks in advance.
